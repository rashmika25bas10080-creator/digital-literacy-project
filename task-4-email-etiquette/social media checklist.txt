Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

 DO's ✅
1. maintain professional profiles on platforms like LinkedIn for networking
2. think before posting—consider how content reflects on you
3. use privacy settings to control who sees your information
4. fact-check information before sharing or reposting
5. report harassment or inappropriate content immediately

DON'Ts ❌
1. share sensitive personal information (address, Aadhaar, financial details)
2. post photos without consent from others in them
3. engage with trolls or participate in online arguments
4. accept friend requests from strangers without verifying identity
5. post negative comments about professors, employers, or institutions
